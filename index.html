The Competition (The Bad Guys)

Before you ask, yes, i know. There are other sites out there where you can create flashcards and quiz yourself. These are sites such as: Cram.com or Quizlet.com (Don't you DARE follow those links!). But what people don't understand is that these websites are too good. These groups have a monopoly on the online flashcard-creating business. They don't give other independent developers a shot at making a good website because they steal all the web traffic.
